## How It Works:

**On Mobile (Expo Go):**
1. Open article details
2. Tap "Export PDF" button
3. Choose between:
   - **WSMAIN** - Full information
   - **WOMAIN** - No supplier info
   - **Cancel** - Go back
4. PDF is generated and ready to share

**On Web:**
1. Open article details
2. Click "Export PDF" button
3. Dialog appears:
   - **OK** = WSMAIN (with supplier)
   - **Cancel** = WOMAIN (without supplier)
4. PDF downloads and sharing options appear
